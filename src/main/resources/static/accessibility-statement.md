# Accessibility Statement

CESSDA is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with
disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for
everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## Conformance Status

The WCAG [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/)
define requirements for designers and developers to improve
accessibility for people with disabilities. They outline three levels
of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. This dashboard is
conformant with WCAG 2.2 Level AA, meaning it meets most
accessibility standards.

## Feedback

For additional assistance or enquiries related to accessibility,
please utilise our [helpdesk](https://helpdesk.cessda.eu/).

## Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

The dashboard's accessibility features are currently undergoing
testing primarily on Firefox and Chrome browsers. The screen reader
mainly used in testing is NVDA. Other major browsers and screen
readers should also be compatible, but some aspects of the website
may not display optimally, especially when using older versions.

## Assessment Approach

CESSDA assessed the accessibility of the dashboard by self-evaluation.
